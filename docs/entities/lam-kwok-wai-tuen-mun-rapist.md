# Lam Kwok Wai (林國偉) - Tuen Mun Rapist

## Overview

Lam Kwok Wai (林國偉) is a notorious serial criminal known as the "Tuen Mun Rapist" (屯門色魔) who operated in Hong Kong. His case represents a watershed moment in Hong Kong's criminal justice history as the first case successfully solved using DNA evidence. Lam's criminal progression from sexual assault to murder provides critical insights into criminal psychology and forensic science.

## Personal Information

### Basic Details
- **Full Name**: Lam Kwok Wai (林國偉)
- **Known Alias**: "Tuen Mun Rapist" (屯門色魔)
- **Nationality**: Hong Kong
- **Status**: Incarcerated (11 consecutive life sentences)

### Physical and Psychological Profile
- **Background**: Tragic childhood circumstances that contributed to criminal development
- **Psychological Profile**: Exhibited characteristics of a serial sexual offender with escalating violence
- **Behavioral Pattern**: Used deception and approach tactics to gain victims' trust
- **Evolution**: Demonstrated clear progression from sexual assault to murder

## Criminal History

### Initial Crimes (Sexual Assault)
Lam's criminal activities began with sexual assaults targeting women in the Tuen Mun district:

**Methods and Tactics**
- Used friendly, brotherly approach to gain victims' trust
- Pretended to be concerned about victims' wellbeing
- Asked about comfort: "冷氣凍不凍啊？" (Is the air conditioning cold?)
- Showed apparent care: "吃了東西沒有啊？" (Have you eaten?)

**Victim Selection**
- Primarily targeted women in the Tuen Mun area
- Used non-confrontational approach to build false sense of security
- Gradually escalated intimacy and violence

### Escalation to Murder
Over time, Lam's crimes escalated from sexual assault to murder:

**Psychological Evolution**
- Initial motivation: Sexual gratification
- Intermediate stage: Need for domination and control
- Final stage: Desire for complete psychological domination
- "不只是要你的身體，還要你的心" (Not just your body, but your heart)

**Violence Escalation**
- Increased control and domination over victims
- Psychological need for complete submission
- Final stage included murder and desire for total control

### Criminal Timeline
